shft

SHFT is about movement.

Between cities, between genres, between ways of seeing. It's a place for music art and culture made at the edges — shaped by borders but never confined by them.

Rooted in borderlands and driven by curiosity, SHFT explores sound, images, and ideas just outside the center. It's where curation and creation live side by side, designed to spark connection and shift how we listen, look, and think.

SHFT treats the margins as a feature, not a flaw — a starting point, not a limitation.

SHFT is a curatorial and creative platform that brings together music, writing photography, and live events in search of new perspectives.

Through concerts, recordings, publications, and collaborations, SHFT creates space for artists and audiences to gather, connect, and reconsider the familiar.

SHFT operates in and between Windsor, Ontario and Detroit, Michigan — two cities separated by a river, connected by deep economic and cultural history, and shaped by movement.

SHFT was founded by Austin Di Pietro, a multidisciplinary artist, musician, and writer based in the Windsor-Detroit region. Much of his work is shaped by crossing between places — geographically, creatively, and culturally.

SHFT brings those interests together in one place, making room for projects that don't fit neatly into one category.

SHFT Presents

A curated concert series featuring original projects and invited artists working across genres and disciplines. Live experiences that prioritize collaboration, experimentation, and cross-border exchange.

SHFT Studios

An independent recording and production space dedicated to original music and collaborative projects. SHFT Studios serves as both laboratory and launch point — a place to experiment, document, and release work shaped by place and perspective.

SHFT Notes

Long-form essays, reflections, and field notes exploring borders, music, culture, and creative practice. Writing lives primarily on Substack, with selected pieces and printed zines extending the conversation into physical form.

SHFT Projects

A home for interdisciplinary and evolving work — from pop-up events to small publications, installations, and experimental collaborations. For the project that doesn't fit neatly inside one box.
